Welcome to ASAP Claening Services

Welcome to ASAP Claening Services….where we like to get dirty!  Let’s be real.  Janitorial service stinks, quite literally in fact, and nobody likes to do it…except us! We (heart) DIRT!  But lets cut the crap (a little janitor humor).  How are we different from every other commercial cleaning company in San Diego?  Our dedication to being real with ourselves, our staff, and our customers while holding true to our core company values:ACCOUNTABILITY, TRUST, and INNOVATION. .

WHO IS ASAP Claening Services?

ASAP Claening Services is a locally owned commercial cleaning company with more than a decade of professional janitorial service experience.

We serve all of San Diego County along with surrounding metropolitan areas. From the shores of La Jolla to the rolling hills of Rancho Bernardo; the Mexico border in Chula Vista, to the border of Camp Pendleton in Oceanside —basically, if you’ve got a business, we want to keep it clean. At ASAP, we take our core company values very seriously.  We set high expectations for ourselves, as a commercial cleaning company, and work diligently to live up to them.  We believe these expectations translate into a culture that is dedicated to customer service and excellence providing quality cleaning services.  Our experienced staff is ready to provide the best janitorial and commercial cleaning services through our commitment to accountability, trust and innovation.  Don’t believe it?  Ask to see the performance evaluations from some of our current clients.
